NIOSH LIFTING CALCULATOR

INPUTS

Horizontal Location
distance from hands to mid-point between medial malleoli measured at origin and destination
Vertical Location
distance from hands above floor measured at origin and destination
Distance
vertical travel = high - low
Angle of Asymmetry
the angle between asymmetry line and sagittal line
Coupling
How well the worker can grasp the object
Frequency
the number of lifts per minute
Duration
the total time spent lifting over a work shift
Load Weight
weight of the object lifted
RESULTS RISK LEVEL AND SUGGESTIONS
Recommended Weight Limit (RWL)
Lifting Index (LI)
Horizontal Multiplier (HM)
Vertical Multiplier (VM)
Distance Multiplier (DM)
Asymmetry Multiplier (AM)
Frequency Multiplier (FM)
Coupling Multiplier (CM)
